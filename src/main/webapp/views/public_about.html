About Us

What Is Echoed?

Echoed makes it easy share your experiences with products you've recently purchased and older favorites that you've grown to love. Snap a pic, write a few sentences and you're done - think of it as a modern day version of show n' tell.

What Can I Do On Echoed?

Create Beautiful Stories: Have you ever wanted to blog about something cool that you bought? With Echoed, you can tell a great story in a few simple steps and have the result look like a million bucks.

Talk To Other Story Tellers: Got questions about a product or want to learn more about a customer's experience with a product that intrigues you? No problem, you can post your inquiries in the story itself and get the discussion started.

Follow Your Friends: Discover what your friends and other customers are buying, sharing and loving through their product stories. We'll create a section that automatically pulls in your social network friends that are also using Echoed.
