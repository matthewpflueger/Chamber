Storytelling 101

Tell Your Story

Once you've logged in, you can share your experience with a great product or service by clicking on the Share A Story button and following the prompts. There's no right or wrong way to tell your story. People want to hear about the the things you love, so snap a pic, write a few sentences and share your story - it's going to be fantastic!

Edit Your Story

You can always go back to the stories you've created and revise them under the My Stories link. If you want to add/edit pictures, descriptions or the story category, roll over any story in your My Stories section and click the Edit button.

Browse Other Stories

The Community is where you can browse and discuss all the great stories that people are sharing. You can browse by category, retail partner or individual storyteller. Just click on what interests you.

We also make it easy see all of your friends on Facebook and Twitter that are using Echoed. Click the My Friends link and anyone in your social universe that's ever shared or commented on a story will be there.
