Collection of Information

Here's what we collect from you when you use the Platform:

  • User-Provided Information: When you sign up or register to use the Platform on our Site or through a third-party merchant ("Distributing Retailer"), we may ask you to provide certain information, such as your such as first name, last name, email, zip code, age, address, gender, etc., including information that is considered "personally identifiable information" (that is, information that would allow us to directly identify you, such as your name and e-mail address). We may collect this information at other times as well, such as when you send us an email, when you update the information you've previously provided to us, and at other times when accessing the Platform.
  • "Automatically Collected" Information: When you use the Platform we may automatically record certain information about your use of the Platform that is made available through your web browser or mobile device such as your IP address, information about your hardware and software, links, images, or other materials you click, information that may enable us to approximate your location, and the date and time you access the Platform. In many cases this means that when you access the Platform, you are not anonymous to us.
  • "Cookies" Information: One form of "automatically collected" information we record is information stored by "cookies." When you use the Platform, we may send one or more cookies to your computer. We use both "session" and "persistent" cookies to collect, store, and sometimes track various types of information. A session cookie is one that disappears after you close your browser. While a persistent cookie remains after you close your browser (and may be used by your browser when you later return to the Platform), persistent cookies generally can be removed. You can review the appropriate "Help" file in your Web browser or your mobile device to learn the proper way to modify your cookie settings.
  • Integrated Service Information: You may be given the option to access or register for the Platform through the use of your user name and passwords for other services (each an "Integrated Service"), such as through the use of your Facebook credentials through Facebook Connect. By doing this, you understand and authorize us to access and use all information that you and such Integrated Services permit us to use or access. For Facebook, this means that upon sign up for the Platform, Facebook should ask your permission to share certain information from your Facebook account with the Platform. Depending on the information you authorize us to access on Facebook, we may have access to a wide variety of information, such as your status updates, photos, videos, name, email address and information regarding your friends on Facebook. You should check your Facebook or other Integrated Service privacy settings to understand and change the information sent to us through Facebook Connect or other Integrated Services.
