The Benefits

Word of Mouth

Consumers know the difference between real and forced messaging. Your shoppers believe in your brand and they are passionate about your products. We provide a service to give that extra push to share this passion with others.

Echoed amplifies the excitement and engagement of your existing customers across a broad network of their like-minded peers. The result is a recommendation from a friend that can be trusted...that's as real as it gets.

Social Networks

The reach and scale of social networks is undeniably powerful. We've created the easiest pathway possible to turn online transactions into brand experiences worth sharing.

Echoed is designed to seamlessly integrate within naturally occurring conversations, connecting with any and all social networks.

Brand Awareness

As products are shared, they receive ample time in the spotlight, inciting conversation and exploration across a consumers entire social network. While this short-term exposure is extremely valuable, we've thought about long-term brand impact as well.

Friends can browse each others exhibits and discover exciting products or their next favorite brand at any time.
