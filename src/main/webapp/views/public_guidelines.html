Story Guidelines

Make It Authentic

These are your stories, which means all content should be yours. Please do not share the images, movies, thoughts and experiences of others.

Common Courtesy Counts

When sharing your story or engaging in a discussion with the author of a story and other community members, please be respectful. Everyone is entiteld to their opinion but let's keep things friendly. Not everyone's taste is the same and that's a good thing - it's what makes Echoed so interesting.

If It Smells Fishy, Let Us Know

We don't condone objectionable content. If you find any materials submitted offensive, please contact us immediately. We will review it and take the appropriate measures. For more information take a look at our Terms of Service

Help Us Be Better

We think that there's something special going on here but your feedback will make it even better! We take your input very seriously, so use the Contact Us tab below to tell us how we can improve Echoed.
