ECHOED + YOUR BUSINESS
Syndicated research has consistently shown that a recommendation from a friend is the most trusted form of advertising for any brand or product.
Word of Mouth
Consumers know the difference between real and forced messaging. Your shoppers believe in your brand and they are passionate about your products. We provide a service to give that extra push to share this passion with others.

Echoed amplifies the excitement and engagement of your existing customers across a broad network of their like-minded peers. The result is a recommendation from a friend that can be trusted...that's as real as it gets.
Social Networks
The reach and scale of social networks is undeniably powerful. Weve created the easiest pathway possible to turn online transactions into brand experiences worth sharing.

Echoed is designed to seamlessly integrate within naturally occurring conversations, connecting with any and all social network networks.
Brand Awareness
As products are shared, they receive ample time in the spotlight, inciting conversation and exploration across a consumers entire social network. While this short-term exposure is extremely valuable, we've thought about long-term brand impact as well.

Every Echoed purchase also lives in the consumers exhibit - think of it as a product gallery, where your products are the star of the show. Unlike other social activity, the exhibit lives forever. Friends can browse each others exhibits and discover exciting products or their next favorite brand at any time.
