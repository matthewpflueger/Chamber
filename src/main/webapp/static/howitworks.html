How It Works Partner Login
ECHOED + YOU
Earn rewards at participating retailers when you share purchases with your social networks.
Share Purchases
Click on the Echo It button to share the item you've just purchased. You can share it in your own personal product gallery or on your social networks.
Spread Word
Echoed will track how many of your friends check out your shared product. As more of your friends click through to the item you shared, the reward you will receive increases.
Receive Rewards
After 7 days, you'll receive a credit from the retailer based on the level of social activity (Clicks accumulated, Comments, Likes, Tweets, Re-Tweets, purchases) for the item you bought and shared. Your reward will automatically be credited back to your original form of payment.
Explore Products
The Echoed Exhibit makes it fun to discover what you and your friends have been buying/sharing. Think of your Exhibit as a beautiful gallery that represents your unique style.
