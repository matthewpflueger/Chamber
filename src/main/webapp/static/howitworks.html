ECHOED + YOU
Earn rewards at participating retailers when you share purchases with your social networks.
Share Your Purchases
Click on the Echo It button to share the item you've just purchased. You can share it in your own personal product gallery or on your social networks.
Spread the word
Echoed will track how many of your friends check out your shared product. As more of your friends click through to the item you shared, the reward that you will receive increases.
Receive Rewards
After 7 days, you'll receive a credit from the retailer based on the number of clicks registered for the item you bought. Your reward will automatically be credited back to your original form of payment.
Go Explore
The Echoed Exhibit makes it fun to discover what you and your friends have been buying/sharing. Think of each Exhibit as a beautiful gallery that represents your unique style. It's easy to navigate and a great way to learn about exciting, new products and brands.
