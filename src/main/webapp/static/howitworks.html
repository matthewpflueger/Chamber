How it works
Sharing Is Rewarding
Echo Your Purchase
After checkout, you will be given the opportunity to share your purchase with friends and receive a reward from the retailer in the form of a credit. There are two ways to receive rewards:

  • Youll automatically earn a percentage off the item youve just purchased by showcasing the product within your Echoed Exhibit.
  • Youll also have the opportunity to receive a bigger reward by sharing the product with your friends


The Echoed Exhibit is the place where you can check out everything you and your friends have purchased - its accessible via our Facebook app as well as Echoed.com. (Click here to see the Exhibit in action)
Rewards get better as word spreads.
You’ll clearly be able to see the window of time and how many clicks from your friends it will take to receive a reward of at least 5% off the item that you’ve bought.

Rewards aren’t for future purchases and your friends don’t have to buy the same item. You receive a credit from the retailer based on the number of clicks registered for the same item you’ve already purchased. No hoops to jump through, it’s 100% seamless.

Better still, the more of your friends that click on your Echo, the greater reward you will receive - in some cases up to 30% off!

Your Friends Check Out What Youve Shared
Your Echoed purchase shows up as posts on Facebook and Tweets on Twitter.

As soon as your friends click on the Echoes, the clicks are registered and count towards the total necessary to hit the minimum 5% off reward.

You’ll be able to track the progress of your reward in real-time within the Echoed app on Facebook or Echoed.com.
