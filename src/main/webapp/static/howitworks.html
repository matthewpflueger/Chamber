How It Works For Business Partner Login
Share Purchases
After checkout Click on the Share button to share the item you've just purchased. You can share it in your own personal product gallery or on your social networks.

Echoed will track how many of your friends check out your shared product. As more of your friends click through to the item you shared, the reward you will receive increases.
Save Money
After 7 days, you'll receive a credit from the retailer based on the level of social activity (Clicks accumulated, Comments, Likes, Tweets, Re-Tweets, purchases) for the item you bought and shared. Your reward will automatically be credited back to your original form of payment.
