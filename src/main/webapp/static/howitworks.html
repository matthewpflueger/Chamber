Spread the word
Echoed will track how many of your friends check out your shared product. As more of your friends click through the item you shared, the reward you receive increases.
Receive Rewards
After X days, you'll receive a credit from the retailer based on the number of clicks registered for the item you bought. You reward will automatically be credited back to your original form of payment.
